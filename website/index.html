About AgriChain

Farm-to-Buyer Traceability & Escrow on Hedera

AgriChain is a blockchain-based solution that creates an immutable record of agricultural supply chain events from farm to buyer. Using Hedera's distributed ledger technology, we provide tamper-proof provenance tracking and automated escrow payments.

Our platform addresses the critical issue of post-harvest losses in African agriculture, which ranges from 10-30% depending on the crop. By creating transparency and trust in the supply chain, we help reduce disputes, speed up payments, and ensure farmers receive fair value for their produce.

With AgriChain, farmers mint tokenized batch receipts (NFTs), custody transfers are recorded on Hedera Consensus Service, and funds are automatically released from escrow once delivery and quality checks are validated.

How AgriChain Works

1

Batch Registration

Farmer registers harvest batch with metadata, photos, and GPS location. System mints a Batch NFT on Hedera Token Service.

2

Custody Transfer

Transporter scans QR code, takes custody, and submits event to Hedera Consensus Service with timestamp and location.

3

Quality Inspection

Warehouse inspector verifies quality, weight, and condition, recording results on the immutable ledger.

4

Escrow Release

Buyer places funds in escrow. Upon delivery confirmation, funds are automatically released to the farmer.
