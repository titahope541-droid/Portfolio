About AgriChain

Home / About

Transforming Agriculture with Blockchain Technology

AgriChain is a revolutionary blockchain-based solution designed to address critical challenges in agricultural supply chains, particularly in developing regions like Cameroon and across Africa. Our platform leverages Hedera's distributed ledger technology to create an immutable, transparent record of agricultural supply chain events from farm to buyer.

At the heart of AgriChain is our commitment to reducing post-harvest losses, which range from 10-30% depending on the crop. This represents a significant economic burden for smallholder farmers who already operate on thin margins. By creating transparency and trust throughout the supply chain, we help reduce disputes, speed up payments, and ensure farmers receive fair value for their produce.

With AgriChain, farmers can mint tokenized batch receipts (NFTs), track custody transfers on Hedera Consensus Service, and receive automated payments through our escrow system once delivery and quality checks are validated.

The Problem We're Solving

Post-Harvest Losses

African farmers lose 10-30% of their harvest due to poor handling, inadequate storage, and inefficient supply chains. This represents billions of dollars in lost revenue annually and contributes to food insecurity.

Delayed Payments

Farmers often wait weeks or months to receive payment for their produce, creating cash flow problems that prevent investment in better equipment, seeds, and farming practices.

Supply Chain Disputes

Lack of transparency leads to frequent disputes over quality, quantity, and delivery timelines, eroding trust between farmers, transporters, and buyers.

Compliance Challenges

Exporters face increasing regulatory pressure, particularly from EU deforestation regulations, requiring verifiable proof of sustainable sourcing and supply chain transparency.

Our Innovative Solution

Immutable Provenance

Every custody transfer and inspection is recorded on Hedera Consensus Service, creating a tamper-proof audit trail that builds trust across the supply chain.

Tokenized Batches

Each harvest batch is represented as an NFT on Hedera Token Service, enabling easy tracking and transfer of ownership while maintaining a clear chain of custody.

Automated Escrow

Our smart escrow system holds buyer funds until delivery confirmation, then automatically releases payment to farmers, eliminating payment delays and reducing disputes.

Mobile-First Design

Progressive Web Apps work on basic smartphones with SMS/USSD fallback for areas with limited connectivity, ensuring accessibility for all users.

Ultra-Low Costs

Hedera's ultra-low fees (as low as $0.0001 per transaction) make micro-payments and frequent custody events practical even for small-scale farmers.

Compliance Ready

Export-ready reports and immutable records help meet EU deforestation regulations and other compliance requirements, opening access to premium markets.
